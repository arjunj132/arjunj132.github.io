Hawaii is a amazing place! It is one of the 50 states in the U.S. It is located in the the Pacific Ocean. It contains 137 volcanic islands.

It was the last state that join the United States of America. It is also the only state that is made up of islands. The largest island's name is called Hawai'i. The capital is Honolulu. The state is also called "The Aloha State". There are 8 main islands in the state.
